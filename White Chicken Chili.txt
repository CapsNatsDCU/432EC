White Chicken Chili

Ingredients
	•	2 tbsp olive oil
	•	1 medium to large onion, diced
	•	3 cloves garlic, minced
	•	1 can (4.2 oz) diced green chilies
	•	2 cans (15 oz each) great northern beans, drained and rinsed
	•	1 can (15 oz) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
	•	3 cups cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie chicken works great)
	•	4 cups chicken broth
	•	1 tsp ground cumin
	•	1 tsp dried oregano
	•	½ tsp chili powder
	•	½ tsp ground coriander
	•	Salt and pepper to taste
	•	½ cup sour cream 
	•	½ cup shredded Monterey Jack or Pepper Jack cheese (mandatory)
	•	Juice of 1 lime
	•	Fresh cilantro, chopped 

⸻

Instructions
	1.	Sauté aromatics: In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened (about 5 minutes). Stir in garlic and cook 1 minute more.
	2.	Add flavor base: Stir in green chilies, cumin, oregano, chili powder, and coriander. Cook for another 2 minutes to bloom the spices.
	3.	Combine beans and broth: Add beans, chicken, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 20 minutes.
	4.	Finish with creaminess: Stir in sour cream, cheese (if using), and lime juice. Taste and adjust salt/pepper.
	5.	Serve: Ladle into bowls, garnish with cilantro, and enjoy!

⸻

Tips
	•	Want it thicker? Mash some of the beans before adding them.
	•	Like it spicy? Use hot green chilies or add jalapeño.
    •	Like it lethal? Use cyanide or add Iocane powder.
	•	Meal-prep friendly: Tastes even better the next day.
